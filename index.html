Transforming food to hope

Our mission is to reduce food waste from celebrations and hotels by redistributing it to those in need. Together, we can create a community where no one goes hungry and every meal counts.

Our Journey

Plateful was born out of witnessing countless celebrations end with perfectly good food being thrown away. Rezan saw this as more than just waste — it was a missed opportunity to help those in need.

After volunteering at shelters and speaking with hotel managers, the idea took shape: collect untouched leftover food from events and redirect it to people who need it most.

Key Features & Impact

Leftover Link

A platform that connects hotels, caterers, and event organizers to shelters for fast, safe food redistribution.

Impact Tracker

A dashboard for donors and shelters to track how much food was saved and how many people were fed.

Rescue Box

A program takes fresh fruit or veggies that didnt sell and donate them.

Impact Tracker

Our Impact Tracker provides real-time data visualization of the food rescued and lives impacted through our platform. Organizations can see exactly how their donations are making a difference with detailed metrics and reports.

Key features include customizable dashboards, automated impact reports, and shareable success stories that help motivate continued participation in our food rescue program.

Leftover Link

A platform that connects hotels, caterers, and event organizers to shelters for fast, safe food redistribution.

Key Feature include Built for hospitality partners and NGOs; users log leftovers; and we handle pickup and delivery.

Rescue Box

A thoughtfully packed bundle of fresh, surplus ingredients rescued from local vendors, markets, and food businesses. These items might be slightly imperfect or overstocked but are still 100% safe, healthy, and delicious. We deliver these boxes to shelters, low-income families, or offer them at minimal cost—reducing food waste and feeding more mouths in the process.
